Immersive / XR

Mural XR Project Canvas

The Problem

Remote collaboration in 2D is flat. Teams were losing engagement and spatial context during complex mapping sessions.

The Move

Designed and launched the XR Project Canvas, translating Mural's 2D stickies into 3D spatial environments where users could "walk" through data.

The Result

22% increase in first-session retention. 40% increase in repeat engagement. Teams reported feeling "more connected" to the work.
